## 🎯 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Quantium Data Analytics Virtual Experience Program**.

The objective was to analyze retail transaction data, understand customer purchasing behavior, identify key revenue drivers, and evaluate the effectiveness of trial store layouts using data-driven insights.

The project follows a real-world analytics workflow used by retail analysts and consultants to support strategic business decisions.

---

## 🏢 Business Problem

The Category Manager wanted to answer the following questions:

✅ Who are the most valuable customers?

✅ Which brands and pack sizes drive the highest sales?

✅ Did the new store layouts improve sales performance?

✅ Should the trial layout be rolled out to other stores?

# 📌 Task 1: Customer & Product Analysis

### Objective

Analyze customer purchasing behavior and identify key drivers of category performance.

### Key Findings

👥 **Mainstream customers** generated the highest revenue and are the primary driver of category sales.

💰 **Premium Retirees** recorded the highest average spend per transaction.

🥔 **Kettle** was the highest-performing brand, generating over **$390K** in sales.

📦 **175g pack size** was the most popular pack size, generating over **$485K** in sales.

### Business Recommendation

* Focus marketing efforts on Mainstream customers.
* Maintain strong visibility for Kettle products.
* Prioritize 175g pack sizes in promotions and merchandising.

---

# 📌 Task 2: Trial Store Analysis

### Objective

Evaluate whether the new store layout improved sales performance and customer purchasing behavior.

### Control Store Selection

Control stores were selected using historical sales similarity and correlation analysis.

| Trial Store | Control Store |
| ----------- | ------------- |
| 77          | 233           |
| 86          | 155           |
| 88          | 125           |

---

## 📈 Trial Results

| Trial Store | Sales Uplift | Customer Uplift |
| ----------- | ------------ | --------------- |
| 77          | 29.13%       | 23.48%          |
| 86          | 9.76%        | 13.54%          |
| 88          | 29.13%       | 22.54%          |

### Key Insights

📊 All trial stores recorded positive sales growth.

🛒 Customer numbers increased across all trial stores.

📈 Stores 77 and 88 achieved the strongest performance improvements.

### Conclusion

The trial layout delivered measurable business impact and supports a broader rollout to similar stores.
